# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Add a site-wide contact information section that includes an email/contact method and an Instagram link.

**Planned changes:**
- Add a clearly labeled public contact area (e.g., in the footer) visible across the site without login.
- Display contact details including an email line and an Instagram external link that opens in a new tab with `rel="noopener noreferrer"`.
- Centralize the email text and Instagram URL/handle in a single exported frontend config/constant and render the contact area from that source.

**User-visible outcome:** Visitors can easily find the photographer’s contact email and click through to the Instagram profile from anywhere on the site, on both mobile and desktop.
